Math Machine

What is Math Machine?

Math Machine is a Terminal script written in Python that does math problems. It can solve one step equations, basic algebra, and more.

Can I contribute?

Yes! We are always looking for people to contribute. To contribute, fork the repository here and make your changes. Then, send a pull request, and one of the team members will look over it.

Can Math Machine do decimal math?

As of right now, Math Machine is not able to do decimal math, although it is high on our to-do list.
